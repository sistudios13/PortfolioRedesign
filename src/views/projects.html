Shotstreak

See Project
Aug 2024

ShotStreak is a basketball shot tracking app that allows users to set daily shot goals and track their progress. Developed with PHP, Alpine JS, HTMX and Tailwind, the app features goal tracking, shot percentage graphs, coaching, and much more. ShotStreak is designed for athletes who want to monitor and improve their shooting performance.
